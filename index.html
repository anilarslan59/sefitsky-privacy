Privacy Policy — Sefitsky

Last updated: April 2, 2026

ArslanLabs ("we", "us", or "our") built the Sefitsky app as a free, ad-supported game. This page informs users regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

Information Collection and Use

Sefitsky does not directly collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The game does not require account creation, login, or any personal data input from the user.

Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. These services include:

Google AdMob — We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data such as device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

Log Data

In the event of an error in the app, we may collect log data through third-party services. This data may include your device name, operating system version, app configuration, the time and date of use, and other diagnostic statistics.

Children's Privacy

Sefitsky is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can take the necessary actions.

Ad Preferences

Users can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting their device settings:

Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
